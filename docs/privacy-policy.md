# Time Secretary Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

## Overview

Time Secretary is a private productivity, time-tracking, reminder, work-hours, secure capture, local briefing, and dashboard notification application.

The app is local-first and stores operational app data locally by default.

## Information Processed

Time Secretary may process:

- Phone number used for SMS messaging.
- SMS command and response content.
- Check-in replies.
- Reminders and todos.
- Location-event text such as arrived work or left work.
- Work-hours records.
- Project notes.
- Secure captures.
- Local briefings and reports.
- Dashboard activity.
- Local configuration values.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- Send time-tracking check-ins.
- Process SMS commands.
- Generate reminders.
- Update work-hours and location records.
- Generate local dashboard reports and briefings.
- Maintain user-requested records and summaries.

## SMS Transport

SMS transport may use Twilio or another SMS provider. SMS message contents pass through the SMS provider and mobile carriers as part of SMS delivery.

Users should avoid sending sensitive work/process details by SMS. Sensitive notes should use secure capture or local app pathways where configured.

## Local Storage

Time Secretary stores app data locally in SQLite by default.

Backups and exports are controlled by the app owner/operator.

The app owner/operator is responsible for securing the local machine, backups, `.env` file, credentials, and database.

## No Sale Or Marketing Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This includes text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Time Secretary does not sell personal information.

Time Secretary does not use SMS opt-in data for marketing.

Time Secretary does not send marketing SMS.

## Third-Party Service Providers

Twilio may be used as SMS transport.

Tailscale may be used for private secure access if configured.

Optional local LLM support is intended to run locally and is disabled by default. Time Secretary is designed to work without an LLM. The app owner/operator controls whether optional local LLM support is enabled.

## SMS Opt-Out

Reply STOP to opt out of SMS.

Reply HELP for help.

## Contact / Support

Support: contact the Time Secretary app owner/operator.
