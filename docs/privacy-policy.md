# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

## Overview

Time Secretary is a private productivity, time-tracking, reminder, and notes application. It is designed for local-first use by the app owner/operator.

Time Secretary may process SMS messages for scheduled check-ins, reminders, time and location confirmations, command responses, and link-only dashboard notifications. SMS transport may use Twilio or another SMS provider. When SMS is used, SMS message contents pass through the SMS provider as part of message delivery.

Sensitive notes, secure captures, and full report contents are intended to be stored locally and should be submitted through the private secure-capture pathway, not SMS.

## Information Processed

Time Secretary may process:

- SMS message text sent to or from the application.
- Time-tracking entries and check-in responses.
- Reminder and command-response data.
- Time and manually submitted location-event records.
- Local project notes, todos, reports, and briefing metadata.
- Secure local captures submitted through the private secure-capture pathway.

Time Secretary stores application data locally in SQLite by default. The app owner/operator controls the local machine, database, backups, exports, and local data retention.

## SMS And Mobile Information

Time Secretary does not use SMS for marketing. SMS messages are operational and may include scheduled time check-ins, reminders, command responses, time/location confirmations, daily summary notifications, and link-only dashboard notifications.

Time Secretary does not send private reports or briefing contents over SMS by default. SMS replies may contain only operational responses or local dashboard links.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This includes text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties for marketing purposes.

## Third-Party Service Providers

SMS delivery may use Twilio or another SMS provider. SMS message contents pass through the SMS provider for message transmission and delivery.

Time Secretary does not sell personal information. Time Secretary does not share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

## Local Storage, Backups, And Exports

Time Secretary stores application data locally by default. The app owner/operator is responsible for securing the local machine, database, backups, exports, environment files, credentials, and private network access.

Backups and exports are controlled by the app owner/operator. Sensitive export content is intended to be redacted by default unless the app owner/operator explicitly enables sensitive export behavior.

## Optional Local LLM Support

Optional local LLM support is disabled by default and is intended to run locally only. Time Secretary is designed to work without an LLM. The app owner/operator controls whether optional local LLM support is enabled.

## Opt Out And Help

Users can opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP.

Users can request help by replying HELP.

## Contact

Support: contact the Time Secretary app owner/operator.
